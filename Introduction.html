Introduction

Thotnot is a local name from the Khmer language living in the South, one of the 54th ethnic groups in the beautiful Vietnamese country. It has been known as a typical product from the Mekong Delta with English name is "Jaggery" or Palm sugar". Thotnot sugar is made from the pistil of the palm trees which are associated with Khmer ethnic group, and people see them as a cultural symbol of Khmer community. Besides the palm tree is also considered a symbol of the patience and hard work of the Khmer.
